Cold Email Script
Send this as your initial outreach. The AI will personalize the highlighted fields based on each coach's program.
Subject: Recruiting Inquiry — [Athlete Name], [Position], Class of [Year] Dear Coach [Last Name], My name is [Athlete Name] and I am a [height] [position] in the class of [year] from [City, State]. I've followed [School Name]'s program closely and am impressed by [specific detail about their program]. I believe my skill set would be a strong fit. This past season I averaged: • [Key Stat 1] • [Key Stat 2] • [Key Stat 3] I maintain a [GPA] GPA. My highlight reel: [Link] Would you be available for a brief call? Sincerely, [Athlete Name]

I remain very interested and wanted to share: [recent update — e.g. scored 24 points in regional semifinal / received interest from two other D1 programs]. Highlight reel: [Link] Even a 10-minute call would mean a lot. [Athlete Name]
